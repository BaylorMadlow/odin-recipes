Deviled Eggs

Description

This wonderful recipe was borrowed from website allrecipes and it's ingredients list is made for 12 servings.

As simple as it is, preparing this still takes time. You should consider how much of it you have left before the party starts, unless you want to find yourself standing in the kitchen at 9AM having impulsively bought 3 dozens of eggs and wondering what made you think you can finish them in half an hour. Because I do not have a clue.

Ingredients

  • 12 large eggs hard-cooked eggs
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ tablespoon and ½ teaspoon favorite flavor mustard
  • ½ tablespoon and ½ teaspoon cider vinegar

Steps

  1. Cut eggs in half and pop out yolks into resealable food-safe plastic bag.
  2. Add remaining ingredients, seal bag and squeeze to mix.
  3. Cut off one corner of plastic bag and squeeze to pipe yolk filling into whites.
